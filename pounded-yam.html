Pounded Yam Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. Yam
  2. Water
  3. Salt (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Peel and cut the yam into small chunks.
  2. Rinse the yam chunks under cold water to remove excess starch.
  3. Place the yam chunks in a pot and add enough water to cover them.
  4. Bring the water to a boil and cook the yam until it becomes soft and easily pierced with a fork.
  5. Drain the water from the cooked yam.
  6. Transfer the cooked yam to a mortar or a food processor.
  7. Pound or blend the yam until it becomes smooth and stretchy, adding small amounts of water if needed.
  8. Add salt to taste and continue pounding or blending until well combined.
  9. Your pounded yam is now ready to be served!
