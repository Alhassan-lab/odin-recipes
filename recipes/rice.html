Fried Rice and Chicken Recipe

Ingredients:

  1. 2 cups of rice
  2. Chicken pieces
  3. Vegetable oil
  4. Onions
  5. Carrots
  6. Peas
  7. Soy sauce
  8. Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Wash the rice thoroughly and cook it according to package instructions.
  2. Cut the chicken into bite-sized pieces and season with salt and pepper.
  3. In a pan, heat vegetable oil and cook the chicken until browned and cooked through.
  4. Remove the cooked chicken from the pan and set it aside.
  5. In the same pan, add more oil if needed and saut√© chopped onions until they become translucent.
  6. Add diced carrots and cook until they begin to soften.
  7. Stir in peas and cooked rice, and continue cooking for a few minutes.
  8. Return the cooked chicken to the pan and mix well.
  9. Season the fried rice and chicken with soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste.
  10. Cook for an additional 5-7 minutes, stirring occasionally, until everything is well combined and heated through.
  11. Your delicious fried rice and chicken is now ready to be served!
