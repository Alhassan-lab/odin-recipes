Pizza and Shawarma Recipe

Pizza Ingredients:

  1. Pizza dough
  2. Tomato sauce
  3. Mozzarella cheese
  4. Pepperoni slices
  5. Green bell peppers
  6. Onions
  7. Mushrooms
  8. Olive oil
  9. Dried oregano

Pizza Instructions:

  1. Preheat your oven according to the pizza dough instructions.
  2. Roll out the pizza dough on a floured surface to your desired thickness.
  3. Spread tomato sauce over the pizza dough.
  4. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese evenly over the sauce.
  5. Top with pepperoni slices, sliced green bell peppers, diced onions, and sliced mushrooms.
  6. Drizzle olive oil over the pizza and sprinkle dried oregano on top.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until the crust is golden and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Your delicious pizza is now ready to be enjoyed!

Shawarma Ingredients:

  1. Chicken or beef strips
  2. Yogurt
  3. Garlic
  4. Lemon juice
  5. Ground cumin
  6. Ground coriander
  7. Paprika
  8. Chopped parsley
  9. Pita bread or flatbreads
  10. Sliced tomatoes and cucumbers (optional)

Shawarma Instructions:

  1. Marinate chicken or beef strips in a mixture of yogurt, minced garlic, lemon juice, ground cumin, ground coriander, paprika, and chopped parsley for at least 1 hour.
  2. Cook the marinated meat in a pan until fully cooked and slightly browned.
  3. Warm pita bread or flatbreads in the oven or on a stovetop.
  4. Place the cooked meat on the bread and add sliced tomatoes and cucumbers if desired.
  5. Roll up the bread to form a shawarma wrap.
  6. Your tasty shawarma is now ready to be served!
